Recovery Flow
BOUNDARY Checkpoint created at safe unit boundary after successful work SNAPSHOT State persisted outside process (URI + hash + format) POLICY Disposition set: RESUME | RESTART_UNIT | ESCALATE | ABORT FAILURE Process crashes or session stops RESTORE Last checkpoint loaded; restore attempt recorded VALIDATE Snapshot hash verified before resume (if required) RESUME Execution continues from next_unit_ref
INVALID or STALE checkpoints block RESUME disposition (enforced). RESUME/RESTART_UNIT require resume_from_unit_ref (enforced).
Validation Rules
NON-EMPTY checkpoint_id, session_id, run_id, agent_id, snapshot_ref_id, snapshot_uri, serialization_format, envelope_id, attempt_id, attempted_at, outcome
NON-NEGATIVE completed_units, remaining_units, size_bytes
RETRY MIN max_restore_attempts must be at least 1
DISPOSITION RESUME and RESTART_UNIT require resume_from_unit_ref
BLOCKED INVALID and STALE checkpoints must not use RESUME disposition
Recovery Disposition Semantics
RESUME Continue from next_unit_ref (requires resume_from_unit_ref)
RESTART_UNIT Re-execute the last unit from resume_from_unit_ref
ESCALATE Send to operator or higher authority for manual handling
ABORT Terminate execution — cannot recover from this checkpoint
