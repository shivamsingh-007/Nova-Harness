Validation Rules
NON-BLANK failure_id, summary, decision_id, envelope_id, cause_id, cause_type, policy_id, policy_name, reason
NON-NEGATIVE max_attempts
MATCH recovery_decision.failure_id must equal failure.failure_id
NON_RECOVERABLE must not use RETRY or RESTART_FROM_CHECKPOINT
RESTART_FROM_CHECKPOINT requires next_checkpoint_id on decision, plus failure.related_checkpoint_id or decision.next_checkpoint_id
Recovery Flow
FAILURE → CLASSIFY (category, severity, origin, recoverability) → CAUSE ANALYSIS (0..n FailureCauseRef) → DECIDE
       ↓
     retry • skip • escalate • pause • abort • restart_from_checkpoint
       ↓
     ENVELOPE ASSEMBLED → cross-validation enforces safe combinations
