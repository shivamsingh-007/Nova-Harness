Validation Rules
NON-BLANK event_id, event_name, envelope_id, attribute key
LEVEL vs OUTCOME ERROR/CRITICAL → outcome=failure; INFO → NOT failure
ERROR KIND EventKind.ERROR must have failure_id in correlation or attributes
SENSITIVE sensitive=True events must not expose password/token/secret keys
LOGRECORD signal_type=LOG, level must be present
TRACEEVENT signal_type=TRACE_EVENT
