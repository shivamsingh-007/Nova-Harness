Validation Rules
NON-BLANK artifact_id, name, uri, provenance_id, lifecycle_id, output_id, envelope_id, run_id
HASH content_hash must not be empty when present
PARENTS parent_artifact_ids must not contain blank entries; order preserved
STATUS-LIFECYCLE ARCHIVED status requires lifecycle.archived_at; DELETED status requires lifecycle.deleted_at
UNIQUE output_ids must be unique within the envelope
ORDERED outputs preserve insertion order
