Validation Rules
NON-BLANK All IDs, scope_ref, unit, and descriptions
NEGATIVE allocated, reserved, consumed amounts cannot be negative
REMAINING remaining = allocated - reserved - consumed (auto-computed)
NAN/INF No NaN or Infinity amounts allowed
HARD hard limit requires non-empty action_on_cross
REALLOCATE reallocate disposition requires reallocated_from_budget_id
CROSS-REF reservations, usage, thresholds, alerts must reference valid budget_line_id in envelope; decisions must match envelope's budget_id
