Validation Rules
NON-BLANK feedback_id, summary, evaluation_id, run_id, rubric_id, rubric_name, criterion_id, description, evidence_id, evidence_type, envelope_id
RANGES score in [0,1] on both QualityCriterion and FeedbackRecord
VERDICT CONSISTENCY PASS must not have all criteria failed; FAIL must not have all criteria passed; UNKNOWN must not have explicit pass/fail values
SOURCE-METHOD HUMAN ≠ HEURISTIC; AUTOMATED ≠ MANUAL_REVIEW; MODEL ≠ HEURISTIC
UNIQUE evaluation_ids unique within envelope
ORDERED criteria, evidence_refs, and signals preserve insertion order
