Decision Semantics
ALLOW Action may proceed immediately BLOCK Action must stop; must not have approval requirement REQUIRE_APPROVAL Execution pauses until human approves; must specify next_action ESCALATE Moved to higher authority; must specify non-NONE escalation_level DEFER Wait for better conditions or more input
Validation Rules
NON-EMPTY decision_id, reason, rule_id, rule_name, condition_id, description, gate_id, envelope_id, run_id, agent_id, actor_id, actor_type
BLOCK BLOCK decision must not have an approval_requirement
NEXT ACTION REQUIRE_APPROVAL decision must specify next_action
ESCALATE ESCALATE decision must specify non-NONE escalation_level
APPROVED approved gate must have approval_actor or approval_note
REF INTEGRITY approval_gate.decision_id must match decision.decision_id
