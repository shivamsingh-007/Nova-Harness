Validation Rules
NON-BLANK skill_id, name, version, input/output ids, permission_profile_id, dependency_id/ref, rule_id, activation_request_id, skill_id, requested_by, decision_id, activation_request_id, decision_reason, activation_id, envelope_id
DEPENDENCY dependency_type must be one of: skill, tool, provider, runtime_feature
RESTRICTED permission profile must have allowlist/denylist when sensitive permissions enabled
STATUS disabled/deprecated skills cannot be activated without override
APPROVAL LOADED/EXECUTED status requires a prior approved decision
CONSISTENCY activation_request_id must match across request, decision, and record
FAILURE FAILED activation status requires failure_reason
POLICY decision_reason is required even for approval
