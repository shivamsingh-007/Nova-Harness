Validation Rules
NON-BLANK All IDs, scope_ref, and summary
BOUNDED risk_score and uncertainty_score must be 0.0–100.0
AUTO-PROCEED requires_human_approval=True cannot have default_disposition=proceed
HIGH/CRITICAL high/critical minimum_risk_level requires requires_human_approval=True
APPROVED/REJECTED requires approver_ref and decision_notes at both record and decision level
CONSTRAINTS proceed_with_constraints requires non-empty resume_constraints
TIMEOUT timed_out approval records require escalation_path with fallback
APPROVER COUNT required_approver_count must be ≥ 1
ENVELOPE requires_human_approval=True blocks auto-proceed without sufficient approved records
