Primitive 18 — Budget & Rate Limits

Hierarchical Budget Policy Contract

Defines what a run is allowed to consume — tokens, cost, runtime, tool calls, and request rates — who the limit applies to, how excess is handled, and what gets recorded when a limit is hit.
