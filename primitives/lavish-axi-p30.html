Approval Flow
TRIGGER Policy / risk / unknown condition requires human approval CONTEXT Subject, summary, risk tier, evidence refs, rollback plan assembled REQUEST ApprovalRequest created with PENDING status and eligible approvers ROUTING Eligible approvers notified (role-based match) DECISION Approve | Deny | Escalate | Override ESCALATION EscalationStep recorded with from/to roles and status RESOLUTION Envelope closed with final decision or expiry
Timeouts fail safe to EXPIRED (not implicit allow). Override requires higher authority role (approver_role containing 'override'). ESCALATE decision must have at least one escalation step.
Validation Rules
NON-EMPTY approval_request_id, run_id, requested_by, subject_ref, summary, approval_decision_id, justification, decided_at, escalation_step_id, to_role, envelope_id
APPROVERS PENDING requests must have at least one eligible_approver
OVERRIDE OVERRIDE decision requires 'override' in approver_role
ESCALATE ESCALATE decision requires at least one escalation_step
EXPIRED EXPIRED request must not have APPROVE or OVERRIDE decision
REF INTEGRITY decision and escalation steps must reference matching approval_request_id
Decision Semantics
APPROVE Execution may proceed (risk accepted by authorized role)
DENY Execution blocked (reason recorded)
ESCALATE Passed to higher authority with escalation step record
OVERRIDE Forced through by explicit override authority (audit trail)
