Validation Rules
NON-BLANK All IDs, party_refs, capability names, gap summaries
COMPATIBLE compatible status requires matched requirements and no unmatched
INCOMPATIBLE incompatible status requires unmatched requirement IDs
VERSIONS minimum_version must be ≤ maximum_version
FALLBACKS proceed_with_fallbacks requires selected_fallbacks and at least one gap
REJECT reject/reroute requires decision_reason
OVERRIDE incompatible cannot proceed without approved_override_ref
