Validation Rules
NON-BLANK event_id, run_id, timestamp, ref_id, ref_type, evidence_id, evidence_type, summary, envelope_id
NO SELF-REF parent_event_id must not equal event_id; superseded_by must not point to same event
REDACTED redacted=True must preserve event_id, run_id, and timestamp for traceability
UNIQUE event_ids must be unique within the envelope
ORDERED events in the envelope preserve insertion order
