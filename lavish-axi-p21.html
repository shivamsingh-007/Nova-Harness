Flow
Agent requests action → risk assessed → approval policy checked ├→ below threshold → auto-allow (no approval needed) └→ at/above threshold → ApprovalRequest created (PENDING) ├→ reviewer approves → APPROVED, executable=True ├→ reviewer rejects → REJECTED, executable=False ├→ timeout → EXPIRED or ESCALATED, executable=False └→ escalated → EscalationRecord created, routed to next role
Validation Rules
NON-EMPTY policy_id, request_id, decision_id, escalation_id, run_id, step_id
NON-EMPTY reviewer_id, reviewer_role, requested_by_agent_id, summary, proposed_action, rationale
POSITIVE timeout_seconds ≥ 1
HIGH/CRIT allowed_reviewer_roles required • reviewer_roles_needed required • evidence required
OUTCOME APPROVED requires final_decision_id • REJECTED/EXPIRED/ESCALATED/CANCELLED not executable
