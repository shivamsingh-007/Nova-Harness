Validation Rules
Non-empty ids run_id, task_id, step_id, tool_name, policy_name, check_name, reason
Non-negative latency tool latency, step latency, total latency
Non-negative step_index monotonic, sequential from 0
Non-negative counts attempts, max_attempts, all summary integers
At least one step RunTrace requires at least 1 StepTrace
finished_at for terminal status SUCCESS, FAILURE, BLOCKED require finished_at + latency
Summary steps match summary.total_steps == len(steps)
attempt <= max_attempts retry bounds check
No secrets or chain-of-thought argument_summary only, not raw args
