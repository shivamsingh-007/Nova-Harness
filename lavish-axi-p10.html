└─ tool_failed_retryable → RETRY_PENDING

Validation Rules
Non-empty machine_id required
Initial ≠ terminal initial_state excluded from terminal_states
Unique terminals no duplicates in terminal_states
No duplicate pairs same (from_state, event) only allowed once
All non-terminal have outgoing every used non-terminal state has at least one transition out
